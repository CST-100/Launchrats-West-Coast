About Us

LaunchRats West Coast and the people behind it.

LaunchRats West Coast was started in early 2018 as a way to get people interested in space travel on the American West coast. We're an offshoot of the original LaunchRats, started by WaywardPlane, based in the Space Coast of Florida. This is currently a mostly one-man project, but we'll be expanding soon!
